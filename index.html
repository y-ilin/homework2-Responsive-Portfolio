This is me!

My name is Yi Lin and I'm a full stack developer in progress! I come from a background in mechanical engineering and currently work as a graduate engineer at a construction company. I'm also currently doing a full stack web development bootcamp with Monash University and will be graduating from that in October 2020.

My experience with coding began with MATLAB in first year of uni, and later brought me to solving really fun problems in a Python class. Somewhere down the line, I stumbled through simulating the fluid mechanics around offshore wind turbines for my Masters dissertation using a C++ toolbox called OpenFOAM. Now, I'm looking to apply my creative side along with my passion for problem-solving in web development and I'm having a sick time so far!
